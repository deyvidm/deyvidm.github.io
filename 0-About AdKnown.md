# AdKnown

The company has a diverse collection of software deeply involved in Advertising. Major projects are aimed at web traffic distribution across platforms, transaction optimization, and streamlined ad targeting and delivery. 

AdKnown also employs a growing team of developers in charge of producing mobile games for Android and iOS. The team's smashing success has led to rapid growth in recent semesters, overflowing the office with game developers. 

At the end of the first co-op semester (late April), the managers were forced to rearrange the office in order to accommodate the team's growth. This resulted in the removal of an essential gap between desks, effectively eliminating a key traffic channel in the eastern part of the office. The whining subsided -- but never quite disappeared -- over the remaining months.
