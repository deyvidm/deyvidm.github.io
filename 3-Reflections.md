# TL;DR: It was a good co-op. 8/10. 

I'm happy with how my co-op experience turned out. 
I worked hard to get ahead and 'catch up' with peers since this was only my second coop (when it should have been my 4th/5th) and feel that I made great strides. 

I was able to leave my employers and managers happy with the work I did, and I was able to grow as a developer and team member. Despite the hiccups and struggles, everything turned out great in the end.
